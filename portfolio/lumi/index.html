The Project

A brand born
from a love of Taiyaki

Lumi started as a personal exploration in 2023 — a fictional Taiyaki brand that needed a full visual identity. Three years later, the design was completely rebuilt: sharper typography, a more refined color palette, and a packaging mockup that feels ready for a real shelf.

The wordmark uses Grandstander — a rounded, playful typeface that balances the warmth of a food brand with the energy of a modern identity. The palette draws from baked dough, red bean paste, and warm wooden surfaces.
